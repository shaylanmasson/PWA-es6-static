String Art

Create a work of art to hang on your wall! Using string and nails make a design, character or object and bring it to life. This is a great kit for parents to help their children be creative and bring great ideas to life. In the kit you will find a piece of wood, nails, a small hammer, design templates (but you can print your own as well) and string. You will want to follow the template to nail the design into the wood, and then wrap the string around the nails to bring the object to life.
